4. Parseval's Energy Ratio Validation

Parseval's Theorem asserts that the total energy computed in the time domain must equal the total energy computed in the frequency domain. Tracking the ratio of these calculations allows the system to identify erratic "clanking" or non-linear impacts that violate standard harmonic models.
